  • [PAUSE] --- ## Section 1: The “Big Three” Framework—Esthetics, Function, Biology (0:45–2:30) When cases get complex, it’s easy to over-focus on what’s visible—shade, shape, and symmetry.
  • Advanced aesthetic dentistry is about balancing three pillars: - **[EMPHASIS] Esthetics**: tooth form, color, midline, smile arc, buccal corridor, gingival display, and harmony with facial features.
  • - **[EMPHASIS] Function**: occlusion, parafunction, anterior guidance, and envelope of function—because a beautiful restoration that fractures is a failed restoration.
