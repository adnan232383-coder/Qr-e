Key Points 1

  • ## Introduction (0:00–1:00) Welcome to **Evidence-Based Dentistry**, Module 1: **Introduction and Fundamentals**.
  • In this module, we’ll build the foundation you’ll use for every evidence-based clinical decision—whether you’re choosing a restorative material, deciding if antibiotics are indicated, or counseling a patient about periodontal treatment options.
  • Evidence-Based Dentistry—often abbreviated as EBD—is not about replacing clinical expertise with research papers.

Key Points 2

  • It’s about combining the best available evidence with your clinical judgment and your patient’s needs and values to make better decisions, more consistently.
