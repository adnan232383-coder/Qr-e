Key Points 1

  • Introduction (Module Opening) Welcome to Aesthetic Dentistry: Introduction & Fundamentals.
  • In this module, we’ll build the foundation for everything you’ll do in cosmetic and esthetic dental care—whether you’re planning a simple whitening case, closing a diastema with composite, or coordinating a full smile rehabilitation.
  • Aesthetic dentistry is not just “making teeth look whiter.” It’s the careful blending of biology, function, and appearance—so the final outcome is attractive, healthy, and stable over time.
