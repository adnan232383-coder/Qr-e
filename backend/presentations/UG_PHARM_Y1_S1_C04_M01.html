  • Introduction: Welcome to Medical Terminology—Introduction & Fundamentals Hello, and welcome to this module on Medical Terminology: Introduction and Fundamentals.
  • If you’ve ever looked at a chart note and felt like it was written in another language, you’re not alone—because in many ways, it is.
  • Medical terminology is a structured language built from word parts—primarily from Greek and Latin—that allows healthcare professionals to communicate precisely and efficiently.
