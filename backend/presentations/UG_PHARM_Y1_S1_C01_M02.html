  • ## Introduction: Why Chemical Bonds Matter in Healthcare and Beyond Welcome to this module on **Chemical Bonding** in General Chemistry.
  • Whether you’re thinking about electrolytes in IV fluids, oxygen binding in the blood, or the materials used in implants and medical devices—chemical bonds are the “rules” that determine how atoms connect, what substances are made of, and how those substances behave.
  • In this lecture, we’ll focus on three major bonding types—ionic, covalent, and metallic bonds—and we’ll build practical skill with drawing [EMPHASIS]Lewis structures[/EMPHASIS], a tool that helps you predict bonding patterns and molecular properties.
