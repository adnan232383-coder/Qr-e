  • [PAUSE] --- ## 2) A Framework for Integration: “Problem List → Objectives → Plan” Advanced orthodontics becomes much easier when you use a consistent framework.
  • ### Step 1: Build a Problem List Your problem list should be organized into four bins: - **Skeletal** (AP, vertical, transverse) - **Dental** (alignment, inclinations, occlusal relationships) - **Soft tissue** (profile, lip competence, smile arc) - **Functional** (shifts, habits, airway, TMJ symptoms) This prevents you from treating “crowding” while missing a vertical growth pattern or a functional shift.
