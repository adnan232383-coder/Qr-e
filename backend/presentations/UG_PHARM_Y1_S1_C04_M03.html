  • Examples of high-yield suffixes: - [EMPHASIS]**-itis** = inflammation - [EMPHASIS]**-ectomy** = removal - [EMPHASIS]**-algia** = pain - [EMPHASIS]**-oma** = tumor or mass - [EMPHASIS]**-osis** = abnormal condition - [EMPHASIS]**-emia** = blood condition - [EMPHASIS]**-logy** = study of - [EMPHASIS]**-pathy** = disease ### Step 2: Find the **root(s)** Roots identify the organ or tissue involved.
